Local Business Website Ratings

For this assignment I have decided to rate the websites of The Game MD, Game Cycle, and The Liquidation Center based on the criteria covered in this course so far. The ratings will be done on a 1-10 system.

The Game MD is a locally owned and operated video game store. They buy and sell used video games, game systems, and accessories. If you are looking for best deals on retro games, Game MD is the store for you! The Game MD also carries new releases and the latest generation of consoles, while offering repairs on your consoles at an affordable price.

Game Cycle

Game Cycle is your one stop shop when it comes to used media! You wanna watch that movie you saw with that one girl like 15 years ago? Looking for a childhood favourite video game? Check out Game Cycle, there's a great chance you will find it for a very reasonable price. Looking to clear some clutter? Take your old CD's, video games and movies to Game Cycle and make some money.

Liquidation Center

The Liquidation Center is arguably the most well-known pawn shop in Windsor. One of the best places to find every day household items below market value. The Liquidation Center buy some of your clutter (if they feel they can sell it). For a college student looking for cheap household items, this is the place for you. Found a new appliance but you already bought the same one here? You can sell those same items right back!
